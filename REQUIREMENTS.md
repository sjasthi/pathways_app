# ICS499 Capstone Project

# Pathways Mobile App

### Discover Opportunities for Middle and High School Students

---

## 1. Project Overview

The objective of this capstone project is to design, develop, test, and deploy a **cross-platform mobile application** using **Thunkable** that helps middle and high school students discover educational opportunities from a curated **Pathways database**.

The application should be suitable for publication on both the **Google Play Store** and the **Apple App Store**.

The initial data source will be an **Excel spreadsheet** containing approximately **1,000 educational opportunities**, including:

* Summer Camps
* Competitions
* Scholarships
* Internships
* STEM Programs
* Leadership Programs
* Research Opportunities
* Online Courses
* Volunteer Opportunities
* Career Exploration Programs

The app should provide an intuitive experience for students, parents, teachers, and counselors.

# 9. Vision Statement

**Pathways** aims to become a free mobile companion that empowers middle and high school students to discover educational opportunities, scholarships, competitions, internships, camps, and enrichment programs that match their interests and aspirations.

By transforming a static spreadsheet into an interactive mobile application, this capstone project demonstrates the complete software engineering lifecycle while creating a meaningful resource that can benefit thousands of students and families.
